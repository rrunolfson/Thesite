# Signal 2 Action

Signal 2 Action is the Last Mile podcast about operational intelligence, semantic context, trustworthy automation, and the path from signal to accountable action.

## Show positioning

- Explores the operational action gap
- Covers semantic context, source authority, quality, and trust
- Looks at how operational signals become organized work and visible response

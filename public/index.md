## Notes

- The Last Mile Platform is the shared foundation for Infinit-Signal, Infinit-Control, and Infinit-Flow.
- Singularity is the shared operational-memory foundation inside the Last Mile Platform.
- Infinit-Signal injects normalized, contextualized operational information into Singularity.
- Infinit-Control draws from Singularity to help customers build their own operational views.
- Infinit-Flow draws from Singularity to trigger and guide workflows using operational context.
- Infinit-Control and Infinit-Flow operate in parallel from the Singularity foundation.
- Singularity retains the resulting operational history.
- Singularity is built on SSOM, the semantic model that helps Last Mile preserve meaning across assets, signals, issues, workflows, evidence, and outcomes.

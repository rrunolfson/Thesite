# Last Mile Integrations

This library contains Last Mile integrations developed for ServiceNow environments. These integrations remain available for organizations that need to connect operational systems with ServiceNow workflows.

The Last Mile Platform extends beyond integration work. It is designed to turn operational signals into shared understanding, workflow, automation, and accountable action across the systems your organization already uses.

## Primary URLs

- Human-readable catalog: https://lastmileinc.ai/integrations
- Machine-readable catalog JSON: https://lastmileinc.ai/integrations.json

## What agents can use here

- Discover currently published integrations by industry
- Identify supported vendors and products
- Find detail pages for products with richer coverage notes
- Use integration API URLs where they are published in the catalog

## Current catalog summary

- The catalog is organized into industries, then vendors, then products.
- Products may include detail pages with coverage summaries, data domains, and key entities.
- The JSON catalog is the canonical machine-readable surface for AI-assisted discovery.

## Recommended workflow

1. Read https://lastmileinc.ai/integrations.json for structured discovery.
2. Use https://lastmileinc.ai/integrations for human-readable browsing.
3. Follow product detail pages when the catalog indicates a detail page is available.
